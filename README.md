## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is a rackmount Ethernet switch?  
A rackmount Ethernet switch is a network switch designed to mount in standard 19″ equipment racks, offering high port density and advanced network features.

### What environments are rackmount switches used in?  
They are commonly used in enterprise, data center, campus, industrial, and service provider networks.

### Do these switches support PoE?  
Yes many models include PoE/PoE+ support to power devices such as IP cameras, access points, and VoIP phones.
